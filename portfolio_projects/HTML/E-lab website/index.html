ABOUT US

Problem

Ivory Coast is the 13th largest mango supplier in Africa, with farmers producing 180,000 tons of mangoes per year but due to the lack of post-harvest infrastructure, they waste up to 40% of their harvest. We aim to provide solar-powered cooling post-harvest storage facilities that will be rented to farmers according to the capacity required, in order to reduce the waste by 7%

Vision X's Mission

Our mission is to provide sustainable solar-powered cooling storage solutions for farmers in remote areas by 2034, in order to subdue post-harvest food wastage.

Our solution

Our solution involves creating a solar-powered storage facility, ensuring efficient mango preservation and minimizing post-harvest losses.

WHAT WE PROPOSE

Our Solution

Our solution is to provide sustainable solar-powered cooling storage solutions for farmers in remote areas in order to subdue post-harvest food wastage.

Implementation Process

1. Solar-Powered Storage Facility: Our solution includes a solar-powered storage facility, ensuring efficient mango preservation and minimizing post-harvest losses.

2. Mango Collection Centers (MCC): MCCs will streamline mango collection from local farmers, improving logistics and reducing transportation delays.

3. Enhanced Market Access: Our solution aims to improve market access for Ivorian farmers by ensuring better mango quality for international markets.

4. Strengthening Value Chain: We 'll strengthen the mango value chain by optimizing post-harvest handling, attracting better prices and increasing profitability for farmers.

5. Sustainable Approach: Utilizing solar power for cooling reflects our commitment to sustainability, reducing operational costs in the long run and promoting environmental conservation.

Impact of our solution

1. Reduces post-harvest losses through efficient mango preservation.

2. Enhances market opportunities for Ivorian farmers.

3. Strengthens the mango value chain, attracting better prices and increasing profitability.

OUR PROTOTYPE

Solar Cooling Facility

This prototype showcases the outdoor design of our solar cooling facility, featuring a wooden exterior selected for its compatibility with the surroundings and cost efficiency. The roof integrates solar panels to supply energy to the cooling systems.

Interior

This is the interior of our cooling facility, featuring insulated aluminum walls and a metallic-coated floor to maintain a consistent cool temperature. Equally features a large interior to accomodate for mango storage

Control Panel

This is the Central Processing Unit (CPU) of our prototype cooling facility. It's a phone-compatible connected appliance that enables farmers/users to monitor and adjust the facility's temperature remotely at any given time.
